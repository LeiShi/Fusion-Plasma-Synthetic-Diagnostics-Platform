Beam Emission Spectroscopy (BES) Directory¶

In order to compare the exact density fluctuation and the BES diagnostic, two classes have been created. A first one the implement the diagnostic (FPSDP.Diagnostics.BES.bes.BES) and a second one that copy the structure of the diagnostic and just take density value at the focus points.
